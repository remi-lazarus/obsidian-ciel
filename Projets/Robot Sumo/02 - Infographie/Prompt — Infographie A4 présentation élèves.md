# Prompt — Infographie A4 présentation élèves

## Prompt

```
Infographic poster, A4 portrait format, for high school students.
Title at the top: "Projet Robot Sumo — Qui veut participer ?"
Subtitle: "Concours de robotique — fin d'année"

Central illustration: a compact autonomous sumo robot (16x16 cm), 
top-down 3/4 view, with a front blade, two wheels, small sensors 
on front, a small OLED screen on top showing a battery icon. 
The robot looks aggressive and dynamic, slightly futuristic design.

Around the robot, 6 circular badges connected by clean lines, 
each with a small icon + short label:
- Top left: brain/chip icon — "ESP32 — Cerveau du robot"
- Top center: sensor icon — "Capteurs laser ToF — Détection adversaire"
- Top right: battery icon — "LiPo 2S + swap rapide — Autonomie"
- Bottom right: gear icon — "Moteurs N20 — Force de poussée"
- Bottom center: screen icon — "Écran OLED — Infos en direct"
- Bottom left: 3D printer icon — "Châssis imprimé 3D — Sur mesure"

At the bottom: a sumo ring (dohyo) top-down view with two robots 
facing each other, one labeled "Notre robot", with a push arrow.

Style: modern tech infographic, clean flat design, dark navy blue 
background with electric blue and orange accents, white text, 
bold typography, icon-based, slightly gaming/esports aesthetic, 
professional but engaging for teenagers aged 15-18.
No photorealism. Vector-style illustration.
```
